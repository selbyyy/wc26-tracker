# WC26 Chances Business Goals

## North Star

By July 20, 2026, WC26 Chances should generate at least $3,000 in net profit from World Cup 2026 search traffic.

The site exists to help fans answer one commercial-intent question quickly: where should I go, watch, book, or follow if I care about this team or host city?

## Time Window

- Build and launch window: May 28, 2026 to June 10, 2026
- Tournament window: June 11, 2026 to July 19, 2026
- Final measurement date: July 20, 2026

## Revenue Targets

- Net profit: $3,000+
- Gross revenue target: $4,000 to $5,000
- Primary revenue paths:
  - Travel and hotel affiliate clicks
  - Ticket planning and ticket marketplace referrals
  - Email capture for team/city route updates
  - City or travel sponsor placements if traffic concentrates

## Traffic Targets

- First milestone: 100 Google organic clicks by June 10, 2026.
- Google organic clicks: 50,000 to 100,000
- Total pageviews: 100,000 to 200,000
- Commercial outbound clicks: 2,000+
- Email leads: 1,000+
- Ranking pages in Google top 10: 20+
- Ranking pages in positions 11 to 30: 100+

## Product Targets

- Every team page answers the confirmed city and schedule question in under 5 seconds.
- Every city page answers which matches, teams, and knockout slots matter.
- Every route page explains possible next cities without pretending uncertain outcomes are known.
- Every high-intent page has one clear commercial action.

## Measurement Cadence

- Sprint: run the `ops/100-click-sprint.md` loop until the first 100 Google organic clicks are reached.
- Daily: Search Console clicks, impressions, CTR, average position, top queries, top pages.
- Daily: Analytics pageviews, engagement, outbound clicks, email captures.
- Weekly: SEO opportunity report and experiment review.
- Weekly: Ship a focused batch of page, metadata, internal-link, or CTA improvements.

## Profit Model

The site should not depend on display ads alone. A realistic path to $3,000 profit is:

- 60,000 to 100,000 high-intent visits.
- 1% to 3% commercial outbound or email conversion.
- 30 to 80 monetizable conversions.
- $40 to $120 profit per successful booking, referral, sponsorship, or lead bundle.

## Operating Principle

Traffic is only useful when it reaches a page that answers a real fan planning question and offers a relevant next step.
