# Weekly Reports

Store weekly SEO and revenue reviews here.

Recommended filename:

`YYYY-MM-DD-weekly-report.md`

Each report should include:

- Top pages by clicks.
- Top queries by impressions.
- High-impression low-CTR opportunities.
- Pages ranking 8 to 20.
- Commercial clicks and email signups.
- Experiments shipped.
- Experiments to keep, revert, or adjust.
- Next week's top 5 changes.
