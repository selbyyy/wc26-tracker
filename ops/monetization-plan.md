# Monetization Plan

## First Commercial Surfaces

The first commercial action should be useful even before affiliate partnerships are finalized:

- Ticket planning: route fans toward official ticket information or a tracked ticket partner.
- Hotel planning: route city traffic toward hotel search.
- Email capture: let fans follow a team or city route.

## Commercial Intent Map

| Intent | Page Type | User Question | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Team schedule | Team page | Where does my team play? | Follow team route / ticket planning |
| City travel | City page | Which matches are in this city? | Compare hotels / city guide |
| Knockout route | Route page | Where could my team go next? | Get route alerts |
| Match detail | Future match page | Who plays, where, and when? | Ticket and hotel planning |

## Short-Term CTA Policy

- Use neutral language until official partnerships are configured.
- Do not claim ticket availability unless verified.
- Prefer "Plan tickets" or "Compare hotels" over unsupported urgency claims.
- Track every outbound commercial click.

## Candidate Revenue Partners

To be selected later:

- Ticket marketplace affiliate or referral programs.
- Hotel affiliate program.
- Travel insurance or eSIM partners.
- City tourism or local guide sponsorships.

## Success Metrics

- Outbound commercial click rate by page type.
- Email signup rate by page type.
- Revenue per 1,000 visits.
- Revenue per page type.
