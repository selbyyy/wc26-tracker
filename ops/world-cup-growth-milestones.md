This plan maps WC26 Chances growth targets to tournament demand spikes. The old
June 10 target of 100 Google organic clicks is now a stretch target because the
site has 80 impressions, 0 clicks, and most impressions still sit on retired
market URLs as of June 5, 2026.

## Practical Forecast

- First 100 organic clicks:
  - Optimistic: Jun 20-Jun 27, if team pages start indexing before second group matches.
  - Base case: Jun 27-Jul 3, driven by final group-stage and Round-of-32 route demand.
  - Slow case: Jul 4-Jul 11, if old market URLs keep absorbing impressions and team pages stay low-ranked.

## Operating Rules

- Before Jun 15, do not overfit page copy to tiny query samples. Fix indexing, redirects, sitemap, and discovery.
- From Jun 16 onward, prioritize teams with matches in the next 72 hours and existing GSC impressions.
- During Jun 24-Jul 3, route and qualification questions should outrank generic schedule copy as the main product surface.
- After each elimination round starts, remove emphasis from eliminated teams unless they have residual traffic, and push remaining-team routes/cities.
- External community promotion remains human-reviewed and should be tied to specific travel or route questions, not generic link drops.
